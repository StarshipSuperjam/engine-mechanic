## Decision

The engine ships its own why: a bounded, law-selected canon of decision records — the rationale and anti-choice behind its structural laws — carried inside the engine as committed contract instances, present from instantiation. Membership is law-selected: founding structural decisions clearing the contract threshold, each entry a pure decision — chosen, rationale, anti-choice, significance — never current-state narrative. The canon changes only by supersession — re-litigating a law writes a new linked record, and a reconciliation folds into the law's existing record — and it is reached on demand by search, scent, or direct read, never pushed into the cold-start pack, with no derived forward edge pulling it into ambient orientation. Ownership splits by engine-owned-set membership at distinct paths: the canon is engine-owned and overlaid wholesale on upgrade, while a deployment's own per-instance records are deployment-authored and preserved — the two are never told apart by a content marker.

## Rationale

A deployed engine's builders can never reach the workspace where its laws were decided, and rationale and anti-choice are authored judgments that cannot be derived — without the canon, a builder retools a law blind. Law-selection bounds the set against record explosion; supersession-only keeps it stable; on-demand reach keeps a stable canon from taxing every session's orientation.

## Anti-choice

Shipping the surface empty was rejected — an engine amnesiac about its own why. One record per planning decision was rejected: explosion and layer conflation. A frontmatter ownership marker was rejected — membership is decided by the engine-owned set, and a marker that can disagree with its folder is a second source of truth.
