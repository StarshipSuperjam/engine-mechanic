## Decision
The thing the engine makes trustworthy is the AI, not a fragile user. The operator is a capable adult who builds through the engine rather than by reading its code; non-code-literacy constrains exactly one thing — trust cannot be routed through code review — which is why the evidence apparatus of audits, cold-session cross-checks, and behavioral demonstration exists. First-read grounding documents carry this role framing, never a deficiency framing, with every hard constraint kept verbatim, including the load-bearing cannot-read-code constraint. Banned-word lists are ended permanently: no word is forbidden and no forbidden-word list may be created; clarity over jargon is judged in writing and review under the operator-communication law's substance edge, never applied as a mechanical word filter — and it is distinct from the machinery leak guard, which is a relevance judgment, not a vocabulary ban.

## Rationale
A deficiency framing, read first every session, regenerated paternalistic copy and divergent forbidden-word lists faster than point fixes could patch; the durable fix is at the root framing. A word filter grades prose mechanically against a judgment-tier expectation and invites unbounded list growth.

## Anti-choice
Rejected curating the word lists, purging accurate descriptive terms, minting a new prose principle, and softening the cannot-read-code constraint.
